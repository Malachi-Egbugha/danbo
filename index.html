ADMISSION PROCEDURE

Danbo International college is a co-educational boarding and day school. To be eligible for admission into year 7 ( JS1) candidate must be 11years old by the year of admission. We also make provision for transfer students. All prospective candidates under-go entrance examination. Examination locations and dates are usually communicated as at when due.

    Application form cost N10,000.00 (ten thousand naira) only. Every fresh applicant should note the following
  • 1. Obtain or fill an on-line or hard copy " Application form" with all necessary information
  • 2. All candidates are to come to the examination venue with the examination slip which will serve as entrance slip to the examination centre
  • 3. Successful candidates are expected to provide the following documents through our admission page or admission office:
    • a. photocopy of candidate's birth certificate.
    • b. 2 recent passport photograph ( preferably in current school's uniform)
    • c. the most recent Academic Report from candidate's current school.
  • 4. Parents living outside Kaduna to provide name and contact details of a Guardian living in Kaduna where possible
  • 5. A provisional letter of acceptance will be issued to successful candidates only
  • 6. The following documents will be given to successful candidates:
    • a. medical form
    • b. check-in list for boys and girls
    • c. list of recommended books
    • d. school bill ( pay advice)
    • e. parents and student data form
    • f. rules and regulations booklets and consent form to be signed by both parents and child

  • INTERIM JOINT MATRICULATION BOARD

    The Interim Joint Matriculation Board was set up in 1974 by Ahmadu Bello University in collaboration with University of Cambridge, United Kingdom. Ever since, the university (ABU) has the mandate to run the programme. The Board has adequately taken charge of the former Higher School Certificate (HSC) Examination that was cancelled in 1989. The IJMB programme like the ‘A’-Level programme is designed to prepare students for direct entry into any university of their choice. This is an alternative route to the University Tertiary Matriculation Examination (JAMB) as a statutory order of seeking admission into Nigeria universities. In other words, students who follow this route need not sit for the UTME before gaining admission to the 200level in any university. Presently, Ahmadu Bello University in collaboration with Danbo International Sixth-Form College prepares students for admission to 200 level of any university of their choice. The duration of the programme is 12 months of 2 semesters. The programme prepares you for 3 combinable subjects leading to your career.

  • CAMBRIDGE ‘A’-LEVEL AND UNIVERSITY FOUNDATION

    Cambridge ‘A’-Level and University Foundation Candidates seeking admission into the Cambridge Advanced Level Program and the International University Foundation courses must in addition to fulfilling the general admission requirements pass at least five ‘O’-Level subjects which must include English Language and mathematics at credit grades in relevant subjects.
